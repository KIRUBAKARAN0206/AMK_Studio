FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the booking policy at AMF Studio?

We recommend booking at least 5-7 days in advance. A 50% advance deposit is required to lock in the dates on our studio calendar. The remaining balance is payable upon delivery of project draft assets.

Do you offer raw files of photography and videography?

Yes, raw footage and raw photography assets are provided as standard in all our custom packages via physical hard drive handover or high-speed cloud download folders.

Can I bring my own gear or external team?

Absolutely. You are welcome to bring your own lenses, cameras, or creative production assistants. Our in-house operators will be present to manage basic studio lighting grids and acoustical console patching.
